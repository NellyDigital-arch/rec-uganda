Background

Rwenzori Environment Connect - Uganda is a community-based organization formed in 2022 by seven founding members. It aims to promote environmental conservation and build strong communities both socially and economically.

Mission

REC-UGANDA's mission is to promote environmental conservation, sustainable development, and community empowerment in the Rwenzori region of Uganda.

Management Structure

REC-UG is managed by two boards:

  • General Assembly: 30 members. Meets once a year to make policies and elect the executive committee.
  • Executive Committee: 5 members responsible for policy implementation, executing activities, and monitoring organization programs.

Main Activities

  • Tree nursery bed making and supply of tree seedlings to public places.
  • Charcoal briquette production project.
  • Crop farming and kitchen gardening project.
  • Community sensitization on environmental management through media programs.
